Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved and iconic dish originating from Italy. It typically consists of a round, flattened dough, often topped with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese, then baked in an oven. What makes pizza truly special is its versatility, as it can be customized with a wide array of toppings, including meats, vegetables, and even fruits. The result is a delicious, hot, and often gooey combination of flavors and textures, making pizza a universally adored comfort food enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to its highest temperature
  2. roll out pizza dough
  3. add sauce, cheese, and toppings
  4. then bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden and bubbly
