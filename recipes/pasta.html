Pasta

Description

Pasta is a staple of Italian cuisine and a beloved comfort food globally. It comes in various shapes and sizes, with the most common being spaghetti, fettuccine, and penne. Pasta is typically made from durum wheat semolina and water, resulting in a versatile and satisfying base for countless dishes. Whether paired with rich tomato sauce and meatballs or served with creamy Alfredo sauce and grilled chicken, pasta offers endless possibilities for flavor combinations. Its simplicity, adaptability, and ability to please a wide range of palates make pasta a timeless and cherished culinary delight.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pasta Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil salted water and add pasta
  2. cook until al dente
  3. reserve some pasta water
  4. drain, toss with sauce, and serve
