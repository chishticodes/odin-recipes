Burger

Description

A burger, short for "hamburger," is a beloved and iconic fast-food item enjoyed worldwide. It typically consists of a seasoned ground meat patty, often beef, chicken, or veggie-based, cooked to perfection and placed inside a sliced bun. Burgers are renowned for their versatility, as they can be customized with a variety of toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, onions, pickles, and condiments like ketchup, mayonnaise, and mustard. Whether savored in a classic diner, at a backyard barbecue, or at a gourmet restaurant, burgers offer a satisfying combination of flavors and textures, making them a universally cherished comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Ground Meat
  • Buns
  • Toppings
  • Sauces

Steps

  1. Form ground meat into patties
  2. season the grill or cook in a skillet to desired doneness
  3. assemble in a bun with toppings and condiments, and serve
